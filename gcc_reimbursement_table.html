Overview: The amount listed for each Chapter represents the total reimbursement for which that Chapter is eligible to receive from Central Office. This amount reflects the cost of round-trip airfare for one delegate traveling from the Chapter's location to the 62nd Grand Council Convention. Each Chapter is eligible for reimbursement for one delegate only, regardless of the number of delegates or alternate delegates attending. Reimbursement is available only to Chapters whose delegate(s) are physically present at and attend the 62nd GCC. Chapters that vote by proxy are not eligible for reimbursement.

Guiding Language — Kappa Psi Constitution & By-Laws

By-Law VI, Sec 1 outlines the purpose of the Grand Council Convention Fund: "The Grand Council Convention Fund shall be used to pay the transportation expenses equivalent to the lowest tourist class, round trip air fare from the Chapter location to the airport of the convention site for one (1) Delegate (not a proxy) from each Collegiate Chapter, each Member of the Executive Committee, and such other Members of the Grand Council designated by the Executive Committee. Any monies remaining in this fund following the determination of anticipated transportation expenses may be utilized to fund other portions of the Grand Council Convention."

Methods

Reimbursement amounts were estimated using Google Flights to identify round-trip airfare to Dallas, Texas, through either Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport (DFW) or Dallas Love Field (DAL). Flight searches assumed arrival by 4:00 PM on Wednesday, July 29, and departure at any time on Sunday, August 2. All available airlines were considered except Spirit and Frontier. Itineraries using different airlines for outbound and return travel were permitted. Consistent with By-Law VI, the reimbursement calculation for each Chapter was based on airfare from the airport closest to that Chapter's School or College of Pharmacy.
